Our Origin Story

The Birth of Based.meme

Based.meme was conceived in a basement at 4 AM by a group of sleep-deprived developers starting to experience hallucinations who were sick of watching their favorite memes get commercialized, sanitized, and ruined by corporate overlords.

Founded in 2025 by a collective of internet veterans who remember when memes were actually funny, Based.meme stands as the last bastion of authentic internet culture.

Our Mission

To preserve the chaotic, irreverent spirit of the early internet before it was colonized by brand managers and content strategists.

We believe memes should be:

  • Painfully relatable
  • Sometimes offensive
  • Never explained
  • Free from corporate influence
  • Gatekept appropriately

Our Philosophy

We don't believe in:

  • Algorithms deciding what's funny
  • Sponsors dictating content
  • Turning memes into NFTs
  • Explaining jokes
  • Touching grass

We do believe in:

  • The chaotic neutral alignment
  • The right to be cringe
  • Gatekeeping as a form of quality control
  • Memes as historical artifacts
  • Your mom
