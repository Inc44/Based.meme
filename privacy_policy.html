Privacy Policy

Yes, we actually have one of these.

TL;DR Version

We collect just enough data to make the site work and to have something to sell when we inevitably get acquired by a soul-sucking tech giant.

Just kidding. Mostly.

What We Collect

  • Your username (we judge you for it).
  • Your email (we promise not to spam you... much).
  • Your IP address (we need to know where to send the pizza).
  • Your meme browsing history (for science).
  • How long you stare at each meme (seriously, get help).
  • The number of times you've exhaled sharply through your nose while browsing.

What We Do With It

We use your data to:

  • Improve our meme recommendation algorithm (it's still garbage).
  • Send you notifications when someone thinks you're funny (rare).
  • Track your descent into increasingly niche meme subcultures.
  • Occasionally show you ads for products you'll never buy.
  • Create anonymous statistics about which memes make people exhale the hardest.

Third-Party Sharing

We don't sell your data because:

  • We're not that desperate (yet).
  • Nobody wants to know what you're laughing at.
  • We're trying to maintain the illusion that we have principles.

Exceptions include:

  • If we get a court order (lol).
  • If someone offers us a truly ridiculous amount of money.
  • If you post something so cringe it requires scientific study.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask us what data we have on you.
  • Request deletion of your data (we'll pretend to do it).
  • Export your data (it's just a text file of all the memes you've upvoted).
  • Complain about our privacy practices (we'll add it to the pile).
  • Leave anytime (but where else will you get your meme fix?).

Cookies

Our site uses cookies to remember your preferences and login status. We also use them to judge your taste in memes.

If you disable cookies, the site will still work, but it will ask you if you're a cop every time you visit. (Cops legally have to tell us if they're cops.)

Security

We protect your data with:

  • A very strong password (it's "password123").
  • A server in someone's basement.
  • A security guard (it's a cat named Miles).
  • Encryption (we think).

In case of a breach, we will notify you by posting a meme about it.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to change this policy whenever we feel like it. We'll notify you by updating the date at the bottom of this page and hoping you notice.
